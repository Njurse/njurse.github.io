Why Support GAIL's Development?

GAIL is built by one passionate developer who believes powerful AI tools should be accessible to everyone. Your donations directly fund:

  • Better Hardware: More powerful servers mean faster responses and new AI capabilities - With enough support, Gail might fold your laundry or do your homework.
  • Advanced AI Models: Cutting-edge models like FLUX Schnell for image generation
  • Free Feature Expansion: Goal is to make more features free as hardware improves
  • Zero Ads: GAIL will never show ads or require channel subscriptions
  • Continuous Development: Regular updates, bug fixes, and new features

Current restrictions on image generation and auto-transcription are purely due to hardware limitations, not profit motives. Every donation helps remove these barriers!
